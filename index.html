Our Story

Redefining Hijab
Modeling in Indonesia

Elmaz Hijab Modeling School was founded with a singular vision — to empower Muslim women with confidence, elegance, and the art of self-expression through fashion and modeling.

Our exclusive program combines world-class training in catwalk, public speaking, personal branding, and photoshoot experience to transform aspiring models into confident, inspiring individuals.
